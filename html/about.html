About

I am a softare engineer coming from a background of mechanical and mechatronics engineering. Throughout most of my professional career, I have been inclined towards creating and using software to improve efficiency, precision, and accuracy. Despite a long history with programming, I never had the opportunity to program professionally. However, time and time again, I found myself taking the initiative to incorporate programming into my daily work. And so, after 4 years as a mechanical project engineer, I left my job to pursue a career in software.

If there is one thing that I love to do, it is create. During my time as a mechanical engineer, I experienced the creative fulfillment of taking a client's functional requirements and designing a product from scratch to meet them. While I still love to design and make physical products, I am now also fascinated by the seemingly infinite number of products that I could create using nothing but software and a computer. I am currently enrolled in Rithm School's web development bootcamp and hopefully on my way to becoming a full-fledged software engineer!

Outside of work I like to hang out with my dog, travel, and play pool. Oh, and I love my food. Whether it be exploring new restaurants, taking on ambitious recipes, or travelling for food, good food probably makes up most of the highlights in my life.
